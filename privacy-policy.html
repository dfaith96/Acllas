Privacy Policy

How Acllas handles personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Acllas College collects, uses, protects, and shares information when students, applicants, families, faculty, partners, or visitors use our website, services, and college-related digital tools.

Information We Collect

We may collect contact details, application information, academic interests, event registrations, communication preferences, device information, and messages sent through our website or college services.

How We Use Information

Acllas uses information to respond to inquiries, manage admissions and enrollment, provide academic and student support, improve services, communicate updates, protect campus systems, and meet legal or institutional obligations.

AI and Student Tools

Where Acllas offers AI-enabled learning or advising tools, we use information to support educational outcomes, improve accessibility, monitor system quality, and reduce misuse. We aim to use AI responsibly, with attention to privacy, fairness, and transparency.

Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers, education partners, legal authorities when required, or institutional teams that need the information to provide college services.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to understand site performance, improve user experience, and support security. Visitors can manage cookie settings through their browser.

Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of certain personal information, subject to legal, academic, and operational requirements. You may also opt out of non-essential communications.

Children's Privacy

Acllas services are intended for college applicants, students, families, employees, and partners. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children where parental consent is required by law.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Acllas College at admissions@acllas.college.
